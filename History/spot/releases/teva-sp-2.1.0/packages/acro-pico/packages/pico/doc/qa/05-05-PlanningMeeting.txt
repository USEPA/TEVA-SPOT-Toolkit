
What: Notes from an internal planning meeting
Goal: Prioritize future PICO development, particularly as it pertains to 
	the upcoming PICO release
Location: 5/24/2005, SNL
Attendees: W. Hart, C. Phillips, J.-P. Watson, J. Berry, V. Leung, B. Carr



Funding/Customers of current PICO capabilities

1. CSRF Robust Optimization
   POC: C. Phillips
   Needs research development focused on robust computing and verification
		of solutions; will support testing
   Funding through FY06

2. CSRF Global Optimization
   POC: W. Hart
   Needs core PICO B&B solver plus LP interfaces
   Funding through FY07

3. LM JSF logistics
   POC: JP Watson
   Needs PC MIP solver running in serial.  Solvers must approximate performance
	of CPLEX (within an order of magnitude), and work robustly (few
	user errors)
   Funding through FY06, possibly beyond

4. FCS Logistics
   POC: JP Watson
   Same as JSF logistics
   Funding through FY05

5. EPA
   POC: W. Hart
   Needs serial MIP solver for Linux/PC.  May also need parallel solvers.
   	Needs Acro support for parallel LP.  Could support branch-and-price
   	development
   Funding through FY06, and probably beyond.

6. MICS
   POC: W. Hart, C. Phillips
   Basic research funding
   Funding through FY06, and probably beyond.

7. TRIPS/OST
   POC: V. Leung
   Needs parallel branch-and-cut and heuristic MIP solvers
   Funding through FY05, and possibly beyond.

8. DAKOTA
   POC: W. Hart
   Needs heuristic MINLP solver using the PICO core.  Needs testing and QA
	facilities in Acro, which are being funded through FY05.
   Funding through FY06.

Possible/unfunded customer interactions

1. Tech Refresh
   POC: J. Watson
   Needs support for branch-and-price and other decomposition methods
   Possible funding in FY06.

2. Eldorado
   POC: J. Berry
   Needs an application driver for multithreaded computing.
   Possible funding in FY06.

3. Combinatorial docking
   POC: W. Hart
   Needs support for PICO core for an existing capability.
   No funding is likely.

3. Bug Management/Performance Refinement
   HIGH
   Activities:
	a. setup Bugzilla reports

   All team members are working on making the use of Bugzilla a standard
	practice

4. COIN Interfacing
   HIGH
   Activities:
	a. resolve differences with the latest version of COIN
	b. resolve legal issues w.r.t. integrating SNL changes into COIN
	c. setup nightly tests of the head COIN release (on tofu)

   HIGH (until the PICO release)

	a. remove Pantex application code from PICO

	c. resolve remaining legal issues within SNL

6. Porting
   MEDIUM
   Activities:

7. Documentation
